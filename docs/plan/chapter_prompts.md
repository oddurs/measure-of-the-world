## Chapter 15: Mean Time and the Equation of Time

### Context

### Technical Focus
- Apparent solar time: defined by the Sun's actual position
- Why apparent solar time is non-uniform: two effects
  - Eccentricity of Earth's orbit (varying orbital speed)
  - Obliquity of the ecliptic (Sun's motion projected onto the equator)
- Mean solar time: the uniform time kept by clocks
- The equation of time: the difference E = apparent − mean
- The analemma: the figure-eight traced by the Sun at fixed clock time
- Deriving the equation of time mathematically from orbital parameters
- Sundials vs. clocks: the dial's "error" is the Sun's truth

### Key Sources

### Narrative Anchor
